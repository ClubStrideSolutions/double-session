Phase 1: Partner Outreach & Initial Material Creation

Tuesday, April 8, 2025 | 1 hour 45 mins

This 1 hour 45-minute session focuses on identifying and contacting prospective local health agencies, community organizations, and school clubs to map out their potential contributions. We’ll begin drafting targeted marketing materials that outline event themes and career exploration objectives, and we’ll conduct a preliminary needs assessment to ensure our workshop design meets community needs.

Session Activities

Click on an activity to see details (Key Terms, Discussion, Activity, Reflection).

Review New Research Materials 30 mins

  • Analyze recently provided data or documents
  • Identify insights relevant to your Draft Marketing Materials
+

Key Terms

Data Analysis: Examining research findings to discover key trends or recommendations.

Research Integration: Incorporating new insights into your new Marketing Materials.

Discussion Points

  • Which findings stand out as most relevant for your workshop’s career exploration or event themes?
  • How can you adapt these insights to different audience groups (e.g., youth, parents, community partners)?

Activity

Highlight 2–3 key points or recommendations you believe could shape your workshop’s content or outreach.

Reflection Questions

  • How do these materials confirm or challenge your initial ideas about the event?
  • Which additional data or resources would you like to explore before finalizing your approach?

Draft Marketing Materials 30 mins

  • Event themes and career exploration objectives
  • Flyer design, social media posts
+

Key Terms

Brand Identity: Consistent look and feel for your event across platforms.

Target Audience: The primary group(s) you want to attract to the workshop.

Discussion Points

  • Which marketing channels (print, social media, email) best reach your audience?
  • How do you highlight both event themes and career exploration in a concise way?

Activity

Sketch or outline a draft flyer or short social media post featuring your workshop’s name, date, and theme. Include a short tagline that emphasizes career pathways or exploration.

Reflection Questions

  • What design elements or messages might resonate with local youth or community partners?
  • How can you measure whether your marketing efforts are effective (signups, social media engagement, etc.)?

Conduct Preliminary Needs Assessment 30 mins

  • Identify key workshop needs or topics
  • Gather initial data from potential partners or the community
+

Key Terms

Needs Assessment: Collecting data to identify gaps in knowledge or resources before finalizing workshop design.

Key Stakeholders: People or groups directly impacted by the workshop (students, parents, partner agencies).

Discussion Points

  • Which workshop topics (health awareness, career building, etc.) seem most relevant based on early partner or community feedback?
  • How do you plan to collect and organize these initial findings (Google Forms, interviews, quick polls)?

Activity

Design a brief needs assessment questionnaire (digital or paper) to circulate among potential attendees or partners. List 3–5 questions that will guide your workshop content decisions.

Reflection Questions

  • What early insights have you gained about your audience’s primary needs?
  • How might your workshop blueprint evolve as more data comes in?

Outcome

By the end of this 1 hour 45-minute session, you should achieve these milestones:

Foundation of Confirmed Partners Ongoing

  • Initial commitments or leads for workshop contributions
  • Shortlist of key partners to follow up with
 

Keep a concise record of each contact, their interests, and potential roles (e.g., sponsoring a booth, providing speakers, co-hosting specific sessions).

Preliminary Workshop Blueprint In Development

  • Rough outline of workshop goals & key themes
  • Early partner roles or potential session ideas
 

Based on your marketing drafts and initial needs assessment, create a skeleton plan for the workshop’s agenda, highlighting career exploration objectives and health-related content.
